## 2. Technical Foundation & MLOps

### What is MLOps?
The discipline of applying DevOps practices to the ML lifecycle to reliably build, deploy, and scale AI systems.

**The Core Lifecycle:**
1.  **Design:** Problem understanding.
2.  **Experimentation:** Data engineering, training, tracking (MLflow).
3.  **Operations:** Packaging (Docker), Deployment (Kubernetes/API), Monitoring.

### Recommended Tech Stack
Focus on **Scikit-learn** first, then **PyTorch**.
*   **Scikit-learn:** Best for classical ML (lead scoring, churn prediction, tabular data). Fast to deploy.
*   **PyTorch:** Best for deep learning, NLP, and custom agents.
*   **TensorFlow:** Optional (only if specific enterprise needs arise).
